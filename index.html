Smoking Gun
A natural distribution should be long-tailed — some standards have 1-2 skills, others 5-10.

The current "93.6% stuck at 3-4" means skill splits are bound by an upstream rule — over-bundled where they should be split, under-merged where they should consolidate.

→ Skill anchoring will be unstable in AI tagging. 自然的知识点分布应该是长尾（部分 standard 1-2 个 skill，部分 5-10 个）。

现状下"93.6% 卡在 3-4 个"说明 skill 拆分被上游规则约束，该拆细的没拆细，该并的没并。

→ AI 打标时 skill 锚定不稳。
